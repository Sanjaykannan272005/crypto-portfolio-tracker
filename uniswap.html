What Is Uniswap (UNI)?

Uniswap is a decentralized protocol that acts as an automated market maker for peer-to-peer cryptocurrency transactions. The Uniswap platform enables users to provide liquidity and swap cryptocurrencies. Its own cryptocurrency, UNI, is used for protocol governance. The Uniswap blockchain is hosted on the Ethereum platform and governed by UNI holders. The Uniswap blockchain is also open source, meaning anyone can view and contribute to the blockchain's code.
